About the Institute

Shalini Coaching Center provides structured academic guidance for school students studying under the CBSE and BSEB curriculum. The coaching focuses on strengthening students’ understanding of fundamental concepts taught in school.

Classes are conducted four days a week and focus on regular practice, revision, and strengthening conceptual clarity. Students receive classroom guidance in core academic subjects in a focused learning environment.
