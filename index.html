About the Institute

Shalini's Coaching Center provides academic support for students studying in CBSE and BLCB curriculum. The coaching focuses on strengthening concepts in core school subjects and helping students improve their understanding and performance in regular school examinations.

Classes are conducted in a focused classroom environment with attention to fundamental learning and regular practice.
