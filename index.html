About the Institute

Shalini Coaching Center provides academic support for students studying under the CBSE and BSEB curriculum. The coaching focuses on strengthening students' understanding of school subjects through regular classroom teaching and practice.

Classes are conducted four days per week and cover the main core subjects taught in school.
